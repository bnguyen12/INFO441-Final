Our names are Benny Nguyen and Sagar Surana, and we’re working on the agriculture/forestry industry. In researching about the potential of strategically planting trees, we found that researchers have used urban vegetation to enhance energy efficiency when it comes to cooling arid climates. Further research into preventing deforestation found that the many countries have put in efforts to restore agricultural land. This research cites that the agriculture industry is the “main driver of deforestation.”

Our mission is simple: allow a user to adopt a tree. However, there's more to it than simply owning a new plant In this day and age, people care a lot about their social media presence. Some buy streets and stars in hopes of gaining likes, reactions, and applauses from their peers because they "own" it. Our inspiration comes from this: humans unintentionally want to boast about what they’ve done. Adopting a tree from our website will give the buyer the ability to name it after them. Not only do they get the satisfaction of bragging about saving the environment, their purchases provide more of an environmental impact than streets and stars ever could. We understand the earnings that we could receive with some success of this project. To even further our purpose of aiding the fight against climate change, we want to be able to use these earnings to help the multiple programs established all over the world that strive for the same purpose. Lyft’s round up program helped us understand that we did not need to set up a single organisation we wanted to help with, but give our users the agency to choose the climate change program of their choice.
